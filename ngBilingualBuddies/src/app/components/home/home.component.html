Learn by Chatting


The best way to learn a language is to actually speak it! Bilingual Buddies connects you with native speakers to chat with for free. But this isn't your standard social app. The interface is packed with innovative tools to make it fun and effortless to connect and learn a new language. You can chat with individual members, you can get a sponsor and language mentor, or join meetups for a collaborative learning experience.
